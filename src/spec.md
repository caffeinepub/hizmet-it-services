# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Integrate uploaded service photos of technician work into the Hardware Repairs, Networking, and Data Recovery sections of the Services component.

**Planned changes:**
- Display uploaded chip-level repair photos (workbench with motherboards, soldering equipment, open desktop computer) in the Hardware Repairs service card
- Display uploaded networking infrastructure photos (server rack with blue and yellow cabling) in the Networking service card
- Display uploaded data recovery photos (hard drives being examined with specialized equipment) in the Data Recovery service card
- Store all 12 uploaded images in frontend/public/assets/generated as static assets

**User-visible outcome:** Users see authentic photos of the technician performing actual service work in the Hardware Repairs, Networking, and Data Recovery sections, providing visual credibility for the services offered.
